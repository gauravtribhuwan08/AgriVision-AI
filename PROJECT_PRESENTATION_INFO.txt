SLIDE 2: HACKATHON PROBLEM STATEMENTS / PROMPTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Our prototype directly solves the following 2 problem statements:

PROMPT 1 (Crop Disease Detection):
"Build a computer-vision powered crop disease detection system that analyzes 
 smartphone leaves images, diagnoses pathogen types, and recommends organic 
 pest control options."

PROMPT 2 (Predictive Soil & Fertilizer Mapping):
"Create a predictive soil nutrient mapping dashboard that leverages satellite 
 telemetry and local weather patterns to optimize fertilizer delivery schedules."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 3: THE PROBLEM IN AGRICULTURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Heavy Crop Losses   : Farmers lose up to 40% of crop yields annually due to late 
                        or inaccurate disease identification.
• Soil Degradation    : Over-application or incorrect usage of chemical fertilizers 
                        depletes long-term soil health.
• Lack of Expertise   : Smallholder farmers lack instant access to agricultural 
                        pathologists and agronomists.
• Weather Uncertainty : Changing weather patterns lead to nutrient leaching and 
                        treatment failure.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 4: THE PROPOSED SOLUTION (AGRIVISION AI)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
AgriVision AI is an end-to-end, AI-powered precision farming platform designed to:
1. Instant Leaf Diagnosis: Instantly detect plant diseases from smartphone photos.
2. Soil Health Analysis : Predict NPK, pH, and organic carbon for any field coordinates.
3. Smart Fertilizer Plan: Generate custom 12-week organic/chemical treatment schedules.
4. Weather Integration  : Combine live climate data with treatment recommendations.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SLIDE 5: KEY FEATURES & MODULES (PROMPTS IMPLEMENTATION)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. AI Crop Disease Detector (Solving Prompt 1)
   - Powered by Google Gemini Vision AI.
   - Analyzes smartphone leaf photos in real-time.
   - Identifies 38+ plant disease classes across major crops (Tomato, Potato, Corn, Rice, Apple, Grape, etc.).
   - Returns disease name, pathogen type (Fungal/Bacterial/Viral/Pest), severity %, and organic remedies.

2. Predictive Soil Nutrient Mapping Dashboard (Solving Prompt 2)
   - Evaluates latitude/longitude field coordinates + OpenWeather climate telemetry.
   - Calculates Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), pH, Organic Carbon %, and Micronutrients (Zn, Fe, S).
   - Generates an interactive 7x7 spatial field health heatmap.

3. Smart Fertilizer Delivery Schedule
   - Generates a week-by-week treatment calendar tailored to specific soil deficiencies.
   - Prioritizes organic fertilizers (FYM, Neem-coated Urea, Bio-mix) to prevent soil degradation.

4. Real-Time Weather Intelligence
   - Integrates live temperature, humidity, rainfall, and wind speeds via OpenWeather API.
   - Adjusts spray advice (e.g., advising foliar sprays vs soil drench based on rain forecasts).
